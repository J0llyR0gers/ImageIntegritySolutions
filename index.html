Ethereum Wallet Trade Activity Analyzer

Analyze Ethereum wallets for the last 30 days of DEX-style trade activity and score each wallet by estimated success vs failure rate.

Configure Analysis

Enter one wallet per line. Uses Etherscan V2 APIs (free key recommended).

How scoring works

  • Filters transactions to the chosen period (default 30 days).
  • Treats txs to major DEX/router contracts (Uniswap, 1inch, Sushi, etc.) as trade attempts.
  • Computes estimated net ETH for each trade tx: incoming internal ETH - outgoing ETH - gas fee.
  • Win if estimated net ETH > 0, else loss. Score blends success rate and net ETH trend.

Note: This is a lightweight heuristic model and not financial advice.
